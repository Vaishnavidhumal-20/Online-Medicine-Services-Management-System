Return & Refund Policy

Your trust is our priority

Return Policy

At PharmaPlus, we strive to ensure that you have a hassle-free experience. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the products within 7 days of delivery, provided they meet the following criteria:

  • Medicines must be in their original packaging, unopened and unused.
  • Healthcare products must have the seal intact.
  • Items must be returned with the original invoice.

Non-Returnable Items

Due to safety and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Temperature-sensitive medicines (refrigerated items).
  • Personal care products if the seal is broken.
  • Baby care products (Diapers, etc.).
  • Wellness products once opened.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund:

Online Payments

Refund will be credited to the original payment mode within 5-7 business days.

Cash on Delivery

Refund will be processed via bank transfer or store credit as per your preference.

How to Initiate a Return?

To initiate a return, follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to "My Orders" in your dashboard.
  2. Select the order and items you wish to return.
  3. Upload a clear photo of the item showing the reason for return.
  4. Our team will review the request and arrange a pickup.
For any further assistance, please contact our support at support@pharmaplus.com or call us at +91 98765 43210.
